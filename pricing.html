Questions

Pricing, answered

Are these prices fixed?

The MOT is a fixed £45. Service and repair prices are real "from" starting prices - larger engines, specialist parts or extra work can add to them, and we always confirm the exact figure in writing before we start.

Why don't you just say "call for a quote"?

Because it wastes your time and hides the price. We publish real starting prices so you can compare us fairly against other garages before you pick up the phone.

Do I pay a deposit?

No deposit for standard bookings. You pay when the work is done, by card, cash, Apple Pay or Google Pay.

Is the MOT retest really free?

Yes. If your car fails and you have the repairs done with us within 10 working days, the retest is free.

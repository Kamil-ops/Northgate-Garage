What it is

What an MOT actually is

An MOT is the annual legal check that your car is safe to be on the road and within emissions limits. It's not a service — it doesn't fix anything — but it does tell you exactly what needs attention to stay road-legal.

We're a DVSA-approved Class 4 station, so the test is done to the government's own standard. The difference with us is what happens after: we show you anything that failed or is borderline, in plain English, with a written quote before any work.

Questions

MOT questions, answered

How long does an MOT take?

About 45 minutes to an hour. You're welcome to wait in reception with a coffee, or drop the keys and collect later the same day.

What happens if my car fails?

We explain exactly what failed and why, in plain English, with a written quote before any work starts. Fix it with us within 10 working days and the retest is free.

Can I book an MOT and service together?

Yes — the MOT and full service combo is our most popular booking and saves £34 versus booking them separately.

Do you test electric and hybrid cars?

Yes. Electric cars need an annual MOT just like any other, and our technicians are IMI-certified for EV and hybrid work.

How do I pay?

Card, cash, Apple Pay and Google Pay. You only pay when the work is done — no deposit for a standard MOT.

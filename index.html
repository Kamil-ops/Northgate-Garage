Questions

Asked all the time

How long does an MOT take?

About 45 minutes to an hour. You're welcome to wait in reception with a coffee, or drop the keys and collect later the same day.

What happens if my car fails?

We explain exactly what failed and why, in plain English, with a written quote before any work starts. Fix it with us within 10 working days and the retest is free.

Can I book an MOT and service together?

Yes - the MOT and full service combo is our most popular booking and saves £34 versus booking them separately.

Do you work on electric and hybrid cars?

Yes. Our technicians are IMI-certified for EV and hybrid work, including battery health checks and high-voltage system servicing.

How do I pay?

Card, cash, Apple Pay and Google Pay. You only pay when the work is done - no deposits for standard bookings.

Is there parking or a key drop?

Free customer parking on the forecourt, and a secure key-drop box by the front door for early drop-offs from 7am.
